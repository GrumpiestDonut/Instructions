# How to Install Phoenix Firestorm Viewer for Second Life

## Complete Nvidia Driver Setup Fedora Process First!
Link: [https://github.com/GrumpiestDonut/Instructions/blob/main/Nvidia Driver Setup Fedora.md](https://github.com/GrumpiestDonut/Instructions/blob/main/Nvidia%20Driver%20Setup%20Fedora.md)

Step 1. Open Firefox and navigate to https://www.firestormviewer.org/choose-your-platform/

<img width="1701" height="888" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/86cbb219-e58e-43f5-bce6-1b6b231989da" />


Step 2. Click Download Firestorm Viewer for Second Life 
<img width="692" height="479" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bd6d68e4-3342-4bb9-8070-d57f0cf9d468" />


Step 3. Click the Tux the Penguin. Yes that is the mascots name for Linux
<img width="2053" height="1320" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d06dd412-bd24-4b9c-b084-331d1483aee5" />


Step 4. Click DOWNLOAD
<img width="1895" height="1315" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4b67bd10-9cf7-40e5-9580-07b6ef2bd25d" />


It will download to your PC
<img width="614" height="348" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9bec4186-866b-4c57-a84e-c7da1073e998" />

Step 5. Open your Downloads folder. 

Either click this button in Firefox:
<img width="685" height="279" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8f4e7328-1b7e-423e-8571-8a6c7c8d08e4" />

OR Click on the Dolphin File Manager:
<img width="423" height="116" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/41ff8593-46d3-4e3d-b6b5-fbb99a27778f" />

And Click the Downloads Folder
<img width="1216" height="680" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/21d3564b-0361-4636-b6e2-3fd8f913bbc0" />

Step 6. When the Download folder opens you should see a Zipped up file for Phoenix Firestorm.
<img width="904" height="681" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d149906b-8f23-47a9-b2dc-b9efce0cd2d7" />

Step 7. Right click on the file, and go to Extract and then Extract here
<img width="903" height="682" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/63c4a04a-a005-4e8e-b8e7-cc6ddd2beb59" />

Step 8. Should put a folder next to it you can click on that:
<img width="895" height="678" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/427fd1a4-2e57-4f07-a400-e1429a6de9ab" />

Step 9. Inside the folder you will find a file called install.sh
<img width="904" height="670" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f39ebeb4-72ea-4a49-b33f-d5096b391515" />

Step 10. Right click on the install.sh file and navigate to Actions then Run in Konsole.
<img width="1261" height="967" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6750a42e-49ac-400b-bb68-d211ecd65a9d" />

Step 11. When Konsole opens you will get the below message. It is telling you that you are about to install Firestorm to the /home/username/firestorm directory. Just press enter

<img width="984" height="409" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e01ea575-25ca-4cee-8a4a-6eba2b1ccf7b" />

Step 12. Firestorm is now installed. Open the Start Menu and under Internet you will find the Firestorm Viewer
<img width="727" height="583" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/17f27955-48bb-4a54-8233-1740bc4b1fb1" />

Step 13. Right Click and select Edit Application... 
<img width="759" height="581" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9b326d81-54ba-44e8-95c3-6841901212fe" />

Step 14. Click Advanced tab
<img width="1086" height="710" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5e7c0b91-22f9-40ee-93f3-ed3e55599e3d" />

Step 15. Check the box next to Run using dedicated graphics card and press Save
<img width="1040" height="705" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/89af291e-f29b-4783-a63a-0d5420850623" />

Step 16. Congrats Firestorm is ready for use. 
<img width="1041" height="776" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a7680599-49e4-4749-8787-4250537f7670" />

Step 17. You can delete any files in your Downloads Folder as they are no longer needed.
